Workout Routines

Unlock training programms personalized for your strength-building needs. There you will know about differnt types of exercise which you start as your routine activity and will be helpful for your fitness. Each of the activity will have detailed description regarding it.

Strength Training

Strength training focuses on increasing muscle strength and size by using resistences. It's beneficial for building muscle, improving metabolism and enhancing overall body function:

  1. Squats

    • Reps: 10-15
    • Sets: 3-4

  2. Bench Press

    • Reps: 8-12
    • Sets: 3-4

  3. Deadlifts

    • Reps: 8-10
    • Sets: 3-4

  4. Push-ups

    • Reps: 10-15
    • Sets: 3-4

  5. Dumble shoulder Press

    • Reps: 8-12
    • Sets: 3-4

Full Body Exercise

This workout strength all the muscles. Doing it can help to to build the good looking body and improve fitness. Aim to do this workout 2-3 times a week for the best result:

  1. Squats

    • Reps: 10-15
    • Sets: 3-4

  2. Push-ups

    • Reps: 8-10
    • Sets: 3-4

  3. Deadlifts

    • Reps: 8-10
    • Sets: 3-4

  4. Planks

    • Duration: 30-60 seconds
    • Sets: 3-4

Lower Body Exercise

This exercise helps focuses on strengthing the muscles in legs, hips and glutes. This exercise are essential for improving mobility, stability and athletic performance. Their detailed description are given below:

  1. Squats

    • Reps: 10-15
    • Sets: 3-4

  2. Lunges

    • Reps: 10-12 per leg
    • Sets: 3-4

  3. Deadlifts

    • Reps: 8-10
    • Sets: 3-4

  4. Leg Press

    • Reps: 10-15
    • Sets: 3-4
